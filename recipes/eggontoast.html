Egg on Toast

Description

You might be thinking, "Sunny, is this really a recipe?" Yes, it is. It's a comfort food.

While being cheap and easy to make, it's also highly customizable. Even your pickiest eater should enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Base Ingredients

    • 1 egg
    • 1 slice of the bread of your choice
    • a pad of butter or margarine
    • salt, to taste
    • pepper, to taste

  • Suggested Toppings

    • crushed garlic chili oil
    • sriracha
    • shredded cheese
    • fruit preserves of your choice (yes, really)

Steps

  1. Prepare the toast

    Put your slice of bread in the toaster for however long you'd prefer. After this, immediately transfer to a buttered or oiled pan and gently sear on both sides. Set aside.

  2. Make the egg

    Fry the egg, in any which way you choose.

    I prefer over medium or scrambled for this application; it allows the eggs to still be jammy but not runny.

  3. Prepare the dish

    Add any liquid toppings onto your toast. Transfer the fried egg onto your toast. Sprinkle on remaining toppings to your liking.

    It's also possible to make another piece of toast and make this into an egg sandwich.
