Tuna Rice

Description

Tuna Rice is really just our household's lazy way of making tuna onigiri.

It's cheap and easy to make, and can be made using a bowl and a rice cooker. It's one of our favorite foods.

Ingredients

  • Rice

    • 3 cups of rice
    • a pinch of sugar
    • rice vinegar, to taste

  • Tuna

    • 4 cans of tuna (in water)
    • 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
    • soy sauce, to taste
    • sriracha, to taste
    • salt, to taste

  • Optional toppings

    • Sesame seeds
    • Seaweed (dried)
    • Bonito flakes
    • MSG

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice

    Put the 3 cups of rice into your rice cooker. Wash the rice until the water runs mostly clear. Flip the switch on the rice cooker.

  2. Prepare the Tuna

    Open and drain the 4 cans of tuna. Combine in a bowl with the 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise, as well as soy sauce, salt and sriracha to taste. Should be pretty salty.

  3. Season the Rice

    Once the rice cooker completes cooking, unplug it. Stir in the pinch of sugar, as well as rice vinegar to taste.

    It should be noted that our house likes a lot of rice vinegar, as the acidity compliments the saltiness of the tuna and the sweetness of the sugar. Add very slowly.

  4. Assemble the Dish

    Combine the tuna and the rice directly in the rice cooker and mix thoroughly. Add any optional toppings you desire.
