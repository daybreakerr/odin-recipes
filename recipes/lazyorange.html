Lazy Orange Chicken

Description

Our family loves Asian food, but we're also not very well off.

As with the other recipes on this website, it's cheap and incredibly easy to make. It's also highly customizable.

Ingredients

  • Base Ingredients

    • approximately 20 chicken nuggets
    • 1 bottle orange sauce, to taste
    • 2 cups of rice, preferrably jasmine
    • MSG, to taste

  • Suggested Toppings

    • crushed garlic chili oil
    • sriracha
    • sesame seeds
    • green onions
    • bonito flakes
    • seaweed (dried)

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice

    Put the 2 cups of rice into the rice cooker; wash until the water runs mostly clear. Turn on the rice cooker.

  2. Prepare the chicken

    Cook the approximately 20 chicken nuggets according to the packaging. Place in bowl and toss in orange sauce.

  3. Prepare the dish

    Mix the rice and the orange chicken together in a bowl. Add toppings to your liking.
